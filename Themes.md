Themes allow you to change the look and feel of your Omeka site.

To change your theme and manage pre-created themes, see the documentation on [managing themes](/Managing_Themes_2.md)

For information on developing and designing your own themes, see the [developer documentation](https://omeka.readthedocs.io/en/latest/Tutorials/index.html#public-themes).
