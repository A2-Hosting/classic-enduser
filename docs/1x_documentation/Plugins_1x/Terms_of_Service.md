<span id="About_Terms_of_Service" class="mw-headline"> About Terms of Service</span>
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Installing the Terms of Service plugin adds an editable terms of service
field that can work with the MyOmeka plugin.

<span id="Installing_and_Configuring" class="mw-headline"> Installing and Configuring </span>
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Download and install Terms of Service (see
    [Installing\_a\_Plugin](https://omeka.org/codex/Installing_a_Plugin "Installing a Plugin"))
2.  Once installed go to /admin/plugins to configure. There are two open
    text boxes for you to write in the terms of using the site and
    materials within and a privacy policy. You are not required to fill
    out both, but you have the opportunity to do so if you wish.
3.  Save changes and then your TOS will be available together with the
    My Omeka plugin.
